How to Play: A Guide to the Game

Getting Started

Before you begin your adventure, you'll need to create your character. The game features a detailed character customization system where you can choose:

  • Your character's name
  • Race
  • Class
  • Gender
  • Core attributes (strength, dexterity, intelligence, wisdom, constitution, and charisma)

Each attribute ranges from 1 to 10 and will significantly impact your gameplay experience. For example, intelligence affects your ability to communicate with NPCs, while strength influences your combat capabilities.

Basic Controls

  • Movement: Use WASD or Arrow keys to move your character around the game world
  • Interaction: Click on NPCs to initiate conversations
  • Combat: Click on NPCs or mobs to engage in combat
  • Looting: Click on defeated enemies to access their inventory
  • Help Menu: Click the Help button in the top-center of the screen to view controls and game information

Game Features

Combat System

  • Engage in real-time combat with NPCs and mobs
  • Combat is based on your character's stats and equipment
  • Critical hits and dodge chances are calculated based on your attributes
  • Stay within range of your target to deal damage
  • Defeated enemies can be looted for items and gold

NPC Interaction

  • NPCs have unique personalities, backgrounds, and motivations
  • You can:
    • Engage in conversations
    • Trade items
    • Receive and complete quests
    • Learn about the game world and its lore
  • NPC relationships affect quest rewards and interactions
  • Some NPCs may be hostile and attack on sight

Effective AI NPC Communication

  • Context Matters:
    • NPCs remember previous conversations and your past actions
    • Time of day and location can affect NPC availability and responses (in development)
  • Building Relationships:
    • Be consistent in your interactions - NPCs notice patterns in your behavior
    • Help NPCs with their problems to build trust
    • Keep promises and follow through on commitments
    • Show respect for NPCs' beliefs and customs
  • Advanced Interaction Tips:
    • Ask specific questions to get detailed information
    • Use NPCs' names and reference past conversations
    • Pay attention to NPCs' emotional states and adjust your approach
    • Some NPCs may have hidden knowledge that requires building trust to reveal
  • Consequences of Interaction:
    • Your choices can permanently affect NPC relationships
    • Negative interactions may lock you out of certain quests
    • Some NPCs may share valuable information only with trusted allies

Quest System

  • Accept quests from NPCs
  • Three types of quests:
    • Kill monster quests
    • Fetch quests (bringing specific items)
    • Kill NPC quests
  • Track your active quests in the Quest Log
  • Quest rewards vary based on your relationship with the quest giver

Inventory and Equipment

  • Manage your items and equipment through the inventory system
  • Starting gold: 300 pieces
  • Starting item: 1 Health Potion
  • Equipment affects your combat stats
  • Trade items with NPCs for gold or other items

Game Interface

  • Combat Log: View your combat history and important events
  • Quest Log: Track your active quests and objectives
  • Inventory: Manage your items and equipment
  • Dialogue System: Interact with NPCs through text-based conversations

Tips for Success

  1. Pay Attention to NPC Relationships
    • Your standing with NPCs affects quest rewards and interactions
    • Some NPCs may lie or have hidden agendas
    • Build positive relationships for better rewards
  2. Combat Strategy
    • Stay within attack range of your targets
    • Use your character's strengths in combat
    • Keep health potions handy for emergencies
  3. Quest Management
    • Check quest requirements carefully
    • Complete quests promptly for better rewards
    • Some quests may have time-sensitive elements
  4. Exploration
    • Explore the world to discover new quests
    • Visit different locations to meet new NPCs
    • Look for opportunities to trade and earn gold

Game World

The game takes place in Grenthollow, a mysterious village located in Agnir, not far from Kadera. Here's what you need to know about the world:

Grenthollow Village

Grenthollow lies at the edge of the Zone of Unstable Magic â€” a small, mist-veiled village that somehow endures while the wilderness around it festers. The village features several important locations:

  • Village Center: The heart of the village where villagers gather, trade, and chat. A small well sits in the middle.
  • Blacksmith's Forge: A hot and noisy forge where blacksmiths craft weapons and armor.
  • Herbalist's Hut: A small wooden hut filled with drying herbs and potions, tucked near the forest edge.
  • Tavern: A cozy gathering place where travelers and locals share stories and drink ale.
  • Market Square: A bustling marketplace where merchants sell their wares and villagers trade goods.
  • Guard Post: A fortified post where village guards keep watch over the surrounding area.
  • Fishing Dock: A wooden dock extending into the lake, where fishermen gather to catch fish.

Dangerous Areas

Beyond the village's safety, several dangerous locations await:

  • Fields: Plain fields to the north of the village, home to wolves and other creatures.
  • Forest Edge: The edge of a dense forest where goblins and boars roam. Travelers report strange sounds from within.
  • Old Mill: An abandoned windmill just outside the village. The locals say it's haunted.
  • Ancient Ruins: Mysterious stone ruins covered in strange symbols. Some say they hold ancient magic.
  • Cave System: A network of dark caves that wind deep into the mountains. Strange noises echo from within.
  • Swamp: A murky swamp filled with twisted trees and mysterious lights. The air is thick with fog.

World Lore

The game world of Agnir is a land of opportunity and danger:

  • Magic is dangerous and often leads to catastrophe
  • Monsters prowl the wilderness, twisted by wild magic and hunger
  • The village of Grenthollow remains strangely untouched by the surrounding dangers
  • Strange disappearances have been occurring in the village
  • Ancient ruins and mysterious locations dot the landscape

As you explore, you'll encounter various NPCs with unique personalities and backgrounds, each with their own stories and quests to share. The world is dynamic, with NPCs and monsters that respond to your actions and choices.

Important Notes

  • The game features permadeath - if your character dies, the game ends
  • Save your progress regularly
  • Pay attention to NPC dialogue for important information
  • Some NPCs may be hostile based on your actions or reputation

Remember that your choices and interactions shape your experience in the game world. Choose your actions wisely and build relationships carefully to maximize your success in this dynamic adventure.
